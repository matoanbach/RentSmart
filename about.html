About RentSmart

RentSmart is a Toronto-based start-up that aims to revolutionize the real estate industry through the power of AI. Our platform uses machine learning algorithms to predict the price and demand of rental properties in the Toronto area, providing renters and landlords with valuable insights and data-driven recommendations.

At RentSmart, we believe that technology has the power to transform the way we live and work. We are committed to providing our users with the most advanced tools and technologies, so that they can make informed decisions and achieve their goals.
